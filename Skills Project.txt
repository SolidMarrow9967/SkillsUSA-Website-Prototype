Website/App Idea

Name:Somerset SkillsUSA Activities Tracker

Features:
 - Event Calander
 - Announcements
 - SkillsUSA Resources
 - Event Photos & Videos
 - Competition Results

Code Features:
 - Accounts
 - Custom Groups
 - Notifications


Pages:
 - Home
    - Welcome message and quick overview of your chapter
    - Featured announcements or upcoming events
    - “Join Us” or “Login” buttons
 - About Us
    - Chapter mission, officers, advisor(s), and history
    - Optional photo of your team or banner
 - Events
    - Calendar of upcoming SkillsUSA events and competitions
    - Option for non-members to see public events only
    - (Members can see detailed info after login)
 - Gallery
    - Photos and videos from past events
    - Option to sort by year or competition
 - Contact
    - Contact form for new members or questions
    - Email or link to social media pages

Members Only Pages:
 - Dashboard
    - Personalized welcome (e.g., “Welcome, Preston!”)
    - Overview of unread messages, upcoming events, and announcements
 - Announcements / Messages Feed
    - Advisors and officers can post announcements
    - Members can react or comment (optional)
    - Push or email notifications for new posts
 - Private Messaging / Chat
    - One-on-one or group messaging
    - Advisors can send mass messages to groups (e.g., “All Competitors” or “Leadership Team”)
 - Event Management Page
    - Members can RSVP or mark attendance
    - Advisors can create/edit events
    - Option to upload files like permission slips, itineraries, etc.
 - Media Upload Page
    - Members upload photos and videos from events
    - Advisors approve uploads before public display (optional moderation)
 - Member Directory / Profile Page
    - List of members with basic info and SkillsUSA role
    - Personal profile (photo, events participated in, contact info)
 - Admin Panel (Advisors & Officers Only)
    - Manage users (add/remove members)
    - Approve uploads and posts
    - Send mass messages or announcements
    - Review analytics (site activity, member engagement)

Optional / Nice-to-Have Pages

Resources Page

Achievements / Awards Page

Showcase medals, recognitions, or chapter accomplishments

Feedback / Suggestion Box
